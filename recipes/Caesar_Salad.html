Caesar Salad

Ingredients

  • Dressing
    • 1 cup - Mayo
    • Juice of 3 Lemons
    • 1 and 1/2 cup - Parmesan
    • 1 tbsp - Worcestershire Sauce
    • 3 bulbs - Garlic
    • 1 tbsp - Fresh Cracked Black Pepper
  • Salad
    • 2 heads of Romaine Hearts - torn into pieces
    • 2 cups - Parmesan
    • 2 cups - Toasted Bread Crumbs
    • 1 tbsp - Fresh Cracked Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. Place all dressing ingredients into a blender or food processor. Taste and add salt or other ingredients to taste.
  2. Place romaine lettuce into a large bowl.
  3. Put assembled dressing into bowl.
  4. Top salad with grated parmesan, bread crumbs, and black pepper to taste
  5. Toss the salad and serve
