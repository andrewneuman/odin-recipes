Vermouth Cream Gnocchi

Ingredients

  • 3 cups - White Wine
  • 3 Chopped Shallots
  • 1 Sprig of Thyme
  • 6 oz - Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 3 oz - Butter
  • 3 tbsp - Vermouth
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 1 package of prepared Gnocchi

Instructions

  1. Star cooking white wine, thyme, and shallots together and reduce wine to 2 tbsp
  2. Add the heavy whipping cream and bring to a boil
  3. Add the butter in small cubes. Whisk well and boil for one minute.
  4. Add the vermouth, remove the thyme, and add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Toss in the gnocchi, cook together for one minute, and serve.
